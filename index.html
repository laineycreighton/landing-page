Lake Tahoe

Keep Tahoe Blue

Lake Tahoe is around 2 million years old and is the second deepest lake in the United States according to the US Environmental Protection Agency. The lake borders both California and Nevada within the Sierra Nevada mountain range and feeds into the Truckee River. Additionally, Lake Tahoe is 99.994% pure making it one of the most pure bodies of water in the world.
